Repair and Reuse

Fix broken items instead of immediately replacing them. Donate or repurpose old clothes, electronics, and furniture to extend their life and reduce unnecessary waste.

Compost Organic Waste

Instead of throwing food scraps and garden waste into the bin, compost them. This reduces landfill waste and creates nutrient-rich soil for plants.

Reduce Single-Use Items

Switch from disposable items like plastic bottles, straws, and cutlery to reusable alternatives such as metal water bottles, bamboo straws, and reusable containers.
