⚡ Save Energy

Switch to Energy-Efficient Appliances

Use LED bulbs, energy-efficient refrigerators, air conditioners, and other appliances. They use less electricity and last longer.

Turn Off Unused Devices

Switch off lights, fans, computers, and chargers when not in use instead of leaving them on standby.

Use Natural Light and Ventilation

Open windows during the day to use sunlight instead of electric lights, and use fans instead of air conditioning when possible.
